# Color Description Count
1 The green colors are Grasses.
2 The orange colors are GrassEaters. They can eat the grasses.
3 The red colors are Predators. They can eat the GrassEater
4 The darkcyan colors are Zombies. They can eat the Predators and GrassEaters.
